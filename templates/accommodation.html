Crash Space

Attendees who will be traveling from other institutions may request crash space. We will find places to stay on a first come, first serve basis. Therefore, if you anticipate needing crash space, please specify which nights you will be staying over on the registration page as early as is possible.

A block of rooms have been reserved at the Quality Inn for check-in May 6th and checking out May 8th, with the special price for a single room at $89 and a double room at $168 per night. Please mention the group name ‘Mayfest’ when booking.
